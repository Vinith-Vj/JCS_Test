KOLLAM HOUSEBOATS

"Kollam Houseboats – Book Now for the Best Houseboat Tours in Kollam. JC'S Holidays offers top-quality houseboat experiences with Deluxe, Premium, and Luxury houseboats. Enjoy reliable service from one of the leading houseboat operators in Kollam – your perfect getaway awaits!"

Kollam is a city brimming with charm, nestled on the banks of the vast and scenic Ashtamudi Lake. A houseboat cruise through these tranquil waters offers the perfect escape—where you can relax, stay, dine, and truly immerse yourself in the timeless beauty of life along Kerala’s backwaters.

The most breathtaking view of this picturesque landscape is from the water, aboard a Kollam houseboat gently gliding through the serene backwaters. These traditional country boats are crafted entirely from wood, yet come fully equipped with modern comforts—air-conditioned rooms, beautifully designed bathrooms, and open lounge areas that offer uninterrupted views of the surrounding natural splendor.

FAQ ABOUT KOLLAM HOUSEBOATS

What is the cost of houseboat in Kollam?

Cost of the houseboat vary depend up on category you choose like deluxe houseboats, Premium houseboats, luxury houseboats etc…..Normal cost for 1 bedroom deluxe houseboat is Rs.10,000/- and there will hike in tariff in season.

What are the different types of houseboats in Kollam?

Cost of the houseboat vary depend up on category you choose like deluxe houseboats, Premium houseboats, luxury houseboats etc…..Normal cost for 1 bedroom deluxe houseboat is Rs.10,000/- and there will hike in tariff in season.

What kind of amenities can I expect?

Starting from single bedroom houseboats to three-bedroom houseboats, you can find plenty of options to book from. While each bedroom comes with an attached toilet, some houseboats even have a living room, dining room and kitchen. Some houseboats also offer private dining rooms, balconies, upper deck, glass walls, etc. Usually 3 staff members are present in houseboats (captain, chef and driver).

What type of cruises are available in Kollam Houseboats?

Overnight stay & day trip are the two major cruises available

Which is preferred- Kollam or Alappuzha Houseboat?

The best to pick will be the Kollam-Alappuzha cruise, which allows travellers to check in Kollam and check out at Alappuzha or vice versa. By choosing this cruise, visitors can enjoy the beauty of both Kollam and Alappuzha backwaters.. You’ll find more houseboat options available in Alappuzha as you cruise through narrow channels. However, there’s heavy traffic in the Alappuzha route. On the other hand, the Kollam cruise will let you have a cruise on a less traffic route. Note: You can also go for the Alapuzha-Kumarakom cruise, which is the longest houseboat cruise in Kerala

If we book the houseboat, are there any other guests to share with us?

No, if you book the houseboat with us, you can enjoy privately. There will not be anyone else to share the houseboat other than the boat staff for your service.
